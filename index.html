SMARTi Assistant | ACO

SMARTi Assistant is an ACO product, it can-
Set a timer
Play a game
Call a person
Compose an email
Order food
Talk to you
Make jokes
AND EVEN WORK OTHER DEVICES! (Turn on lights, TV, or any other smart device) When you fire up the app for the first time a cool "quiz" is there, fill it in with your choices like the radio station you listen to, what your main email is, and what color you want the app backround to be! From that info the app will customize everything for you, like if you chose blue app, the backround of SMARTi will become blue, and if you said ACO news is your prime news station, when you ask for news, it will only give you ACO news.

More setup
If you want to step up and add more cool things to your SMARTi, you make add a morning path, this means everytime you wake up and ask for a morning path, it will give you the custom morning path. You can add things like "Read my emails to me", or "give me a joke to start the day" and it will automaticaly give you all that in the morning.

Addons
ACO Also provides addons to the free AI, some addons are free as some may cost 1-5$, (ACO premium would also get you that for free) These plugins can let you change SMARTi's logo and app icon, some free addons let you change the voice of SMARTi. Our name addon lets you add peoples voice to SMARTi and it will detect the voice of the person whenver the used!

Security
You can add fingerprint and face before you are able to use SMARTi, you can also add a password before sending emails or calling a person so it will not accidently happen. We also have pocket click prevention so the phone wont do anything if you leave it in your pocket or if you baby brother starts clicking all over your phone! You can also add a child lock for kids so they are not able to call using your SMARTi app.
